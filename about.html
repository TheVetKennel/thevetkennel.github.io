About The Vet Kennel

Meet the creator and the mission behind the site.

Hey! I’m Lucy — a UK-qualified veterinarian passionate about education, wellness, and accessible resources for vet students and new grads.

The Vet Kennel is my creative outlet: a mix of storytelling, study tools, and simple explanations for complex clinical concepts. Whether you're revising for NAVLE, creating Anki decks, or just love good formatting, you're in the right place.

Thanks for being here — and I hope it helps you feel a little more confident on your vetmed journey.
